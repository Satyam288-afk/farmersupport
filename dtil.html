Problem: Crop Disease & Pest Management

Many farmers struggle with crop diseases and pests that reduce their yields. Without effective and timely treatment, these issues can greatly impact productivity.

Solution

  • Use organic pesticides that are safe for crops and the environment.
  • Rotate crops to minimize disease build-up in the soil.
  • Monitor crops regularly to detect early signs of infestation.
  • Implement integrated pest management (IPM) for sustainable control.
